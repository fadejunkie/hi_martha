About Arquero Co

From San Miguel to the shop floor

Arquero Co is the bridge between heritage and hard work. We honor Latino identity, family trade knowledge, and blue-collar grit with gear made to last.

A timeline in steel and smoke

2017 — First sparks

Started with custom caps for local welders and fitters in South Texas yards.

2019 — Heritage line launch

San Miguel collection debuted, celebrating roots through iconography and storytelling.

2022 — Field-tested expansion

Added FR bandanas, tool roll pouches, and work-ready accessories shaped by customer feedback.

Today — Aim True. Weld True.

Arquero Co continues building premium lifestyle gear for the people who keep industry moving.
